Welcome to Selena Gomez's Page!

Selena Gomez is a multi-talented musician, actress, and producer. She began her career as a child actress on the hit TV show "Barney & Friends", and later rose to fame as a musician with her band Selena Gomez & the Scene.

Biography

Selena Marie Gomez was born on July 22, 1992 in Grand Prairie, Texas. She began her career as a child actress on the hit TV show "Barney & Friends" and later landed her breakthrough role as Alex Russo in the Disney Channel series "Wizards of Waverly Place".

In addition to her acting career, Selena has released several successful albums as a musician, including "Stars Dance", "Revival", and "Rare". She has won several awards for her music, including an American Music Award, a Billboard Women in Music award, and an iHeartRadio Music award.
